Woodworking quests teach you to turn raw lumber into durable objects. The focus is on safe cuts,
solid joinery, and finishes that protect and extend the life of your builds.

## What you'll practice

- **Cutting and shaping** — saw lumber to size and clean up edges with chisels.
- **Assembly** — glue panels, clamp joints, and square up frames.
- **Finishing** — sand, stain, and seal for durability.

## Inventory and tools to expect

Woodworking inventory includes:

- Workbenches, hand saws, and wood chisels
- Wood glue, sandpaper, and paint brushes
- Pre-cut panel kits for birdhouses and planter boxes

## Safety reminders

- Clamp material before cutting to keep your hands clear.
- Wear eye protection when sanding or sawing.
