Woodworking in DSPACE is about building practical structures and learning safe finishing
processes. The quests move from sanding and finishing to assembling functional furniture.

## What you will practice

- **Surface prep**: sanding, cleaning, and edge treatment.
- **Assembly**: joining panels and aligning components.
- **Finishing**: applying sealants and protecting wood from moisture.
- **Project planning**: building small structures before moving to furniture.

## Quest flow highlights

Woodworking quests cover birdhouse assembly, small bookshelves, coffee tables, and finishing
passes. The emphasis is on clean joints and durable surfaces.

## Inventory highlights

- Birdhouse panel sets, pine boards, and wood-safe adhesives.
- Finished planter boxes and project checklists.

## Why it matters

Woodworking gives you the ability to create shelters, storage, and tools locally. It pairs
well with 3D printing for hybrid fabrication.
