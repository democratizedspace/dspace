Woodworking quests build practical shop skills for constructing durable structures and tools.
You will measure, cut, assemble, sand, and finish projects while tracking safety practices.

## What this skill covers

- Measuring, marking, and planning cuts accurately.
- Assembling joints and ensuring square, level builds.
- Sanding and finishing for durability and appearance.
- Building useful objects like benches, racks, and planters.

## Recommended inventory

- Tape measure, hammer, and clamps for assembly work.
- Sanding blocks or sandpaper for finishing passes.
- Paintbrushes or rags for stains and protective coatings.

## Quest highlights

- [Birdhouse](/quests/woodworking/birdhouse) introduces basic assembly.
- [Step stool](/quests/woodworking/step-stool) builds sturdier joints.
- [Workbench](/quests/woodworking/workbench) is the flagship build.
- [Apply finish](/quests/woodworking/apply-finish) seals and protects projects.

## Best practices

1. Measure twice, cut once, and keep blades sharp.
2. Use PPE and keep the workspace clear of offcuts.
3. Dry-fit assemblies before applying glue or fasteners.
4. Let finishes fully cure before heavy use.

## Where to go next

Woodworking pairs well with composting and aquaria projects that need sturdy stands or bins.
