Electricity in DSPACE is a resource you generate, store, and spend. The game tracks the
carbon cost of non-renewable power and rewards you for building clean, resilient systems.

## Non-renewable energy and dCarbon

When you use grid power, you accrue [dCarbon](/docs/dCarbon). For every 1,000 dCarbon
produced, your sell prices drop by 1% (up to a 90% reduction). This creates a real
incentive to shift to renewable generation and efficient storage.

## Renewable power and dWatt

Renewable energy produces [dWatt](/docs/dwatt), the in-game unit of usable electricity.
Solar panels, wind turbines, and hand-crank generators let you create dWatt without the
carbon penalty.

## What you will practice

- **Generation**: using solar panels, wind turbines, and crank generators.
- **Storage**: maintaining battery packs and monitoring charge controllers.
- **Conversion**: safely running inverters for AC loads.
- **Efficiency**: measuring loads and prioritizing essential systems.

## Inventory highlights

- Portable solar panels, 500 W wind turbines, and hand-crank generator assemblies.
- 200 Wh and 1 kWh battery packs with fused DC leads.
- Pure sine inverters and load-test logs for safe AC power.
- Tokens like dSolar, dWind, and dWatt to track generation paths.
