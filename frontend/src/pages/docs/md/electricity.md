Electricity in DSPACE spans safe power usage, basic electronics, and the carbon accountability
layer that sits on top of grid power. The goal is to help you build confidence with circuits
while nudging you toward clean energy.

## Power economics

If you plug a machine into grid power, DSPACE applies a built-in carbon tax. For every 1000
[dCarbon](/docs/dCarbon) produced within the game, your sell prices in the shop drop by 1%, up to
a maximum reduction of 90%. This creates a real incentive to adopt renewable energy sources.

## What you'll practice

- **Measurement** — verify voltage, resistance, and continuity before powering a circuit.
- **Circuit basics** — learn LED polarity, voltage dividers, and safe wiring habits.
- **Safety** — test GFCI outlets and treat mains power with respect.

Electronics workflows lean on staple gear like:
