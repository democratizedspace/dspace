Guilds are not live yet. They are planned for 2027 or later, after the
multiplayer foundation ships. The roadmap puts guilds in a future milestone so
players have time to shape how collaboration should work before it goes live.

## What will enable guilds

Guilds will arrive once ActivityPub integration is in place. ActivityPub will
let DSPACE connect accounts and social activity across instances, which we plan
to use as the backbone for guild creation, membership, and shared progression.

## Planned guild experience

- **Player-created guilds** – Members will be able to create their own guilds,
  choose names and tags, and recruit collaborators.
- **Guild-specific content** – Guild quests, inventories, and milestones will
  offer in-game benefits such as shared tooling, bonuses, and unlocks.
- **Social proof** – ActivityPub will let achievements, contributions, and
  guild activity travel with you across servers.

## Metaguild structure

The Metaguild is the planned flagship guild. It is not recruiting yet, but the
structure below outlines how we expect it to work once guilds go live.

- **Specialist wings** – Sydney, Hydro, Nova, and the rest of the persona
  roster will lead discipline-focused wings (print farm, hydroponics, rocketry,
  medicine, and more). Their quests will unlock tooling, safety checklists, and
  ongoing maintenance tasks for the whole crew.
- **Donation board** – The [Leaderboard](/leaderboard) is a preview of the
  future donation board that will power the universal basic income pool. It is
  not tied to guild membership yet.
- **Operations log** – The [Stats](/stats) destination and quest trees will
  highlight the shared progress the Metaguild catalogs, keeping everyone
  aligned on what content ships next.

## How to prepare now

1.  Follow the [roadmap](/docs/roadmap) for the latest timeline updates.
2.  Explore the "Universal Basic Income" quest line to learn how the
    contribution loop is expected to work once guilds launch.
3.  Chat with NPC specialists such as Sydney or Hydro through dChat for
    guidance on current quests, even though guild membership is not available
    yet.
