# About DSPACE

DSPACE is an incremental simulation game about resource management and practical systems thinking
for long-term space civilization goals. v3 focuses on grounded, teachable loops: quests,
inventory, processes, and player-authored content.

## What v3 emphasizes

- **Learn-by-building**: complete quests tied to real-world-inspired workflows.
- **Systems literacy**: connect energy, fabrication, life support, and operations.
- **Creator tooling**: build your own quests/items/processes directly in-game.
- **Resilient saves**: IndexedDB-first storage with backups, cloud sync, and migration helpers.

## Current gameplay scope

The active catalog covers onboarding, energy systems, electronics, hydroponics, aquaria,
chemistry, programming, robotics, rocketry, operations, and more. Rocketry content is model-scale
and education-oriented in v3.

Browse [Quest Trees](/docs/quest-trees) to choose a learning path.

## Long-term direction

Future releases expand toward deeper off-world systems, advanced operations, and multiplayer/guild
mechanics. v3 builds the foundations first so those systems are coherent when they arrive.
