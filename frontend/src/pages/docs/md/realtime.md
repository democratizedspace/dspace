---
title: 'Everything is real-time'
slug: 'realtime'
---

At v3, everything in the game is real-time.

If a [3d print](/docs/3dprinting) (or any other machine or [process](/docs/processes))
takes 1 hour in the real world, it takes 1 hour in the game. Flying to Mars with
traditional propulsive rockets still takes a whole 5 months at first!

We chose real-time progression to keep processes (and therefore quests) grounded in the
world's logic, so the timing feels diegetic instead of gamified. By slowing things down to
real-time, we ask players to make critical decisions about what they want to do and plan
accordingly. It also makes the game more accessible to people who don't have a lot of time
to play, as they can set and forget some long-running process.
