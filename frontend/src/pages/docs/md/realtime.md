---
title: 'Everything is real-time'
slug: 'realtime'
---

At v3, everything in the game runs in real time.

If a [3d print](/docs/3dprinting) (or any other machine or [process](/docs/processes)) takes 1 hour in the real world, it will take 1 hour in the game. Flying to Mars with traditional propulsive rockets will take a whole 5 months at first!

Keeping progression real-time makes processes feel diegetic, so quests inherit that
grounded pacing too. It forces players to make critical decisions about what they want to
do and plan accordingly, while still being accessible to people who don't have a lot of
time to play because they can set and forget a long-running process.
