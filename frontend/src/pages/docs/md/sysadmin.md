System administration in DSPACE is about keeping machines healthy: reading logs, monitoring
resources, and understanding the command line.

## What you will practice

- **Core commands**: navigating filesystems and inspecting system state.
- **Observability**: reading logs and monitoring CPU, memory, and disk usage.
- **Operations hygiene**: keeping systems clean, updated, and documented.

## Quest flow highlights

Sysadmin quests teach basic Linux commands, system log inspection, and resource monitoring.
They are the foundation for the more advanced DevOps quests.

## Inventory highlights

- Raspberry Pi cluster nodes and monitoring artifacts.
- Log snapshots and system health checklists.

## Why it matters

Every DSPACE system depends on reliable hosts. Strong sysadmin habits prevent outages and
make troubleshooting faster.
