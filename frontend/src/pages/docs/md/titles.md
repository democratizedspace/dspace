Titles celebrate the most prestigious milestones in your save file. Each rank is tied to a high-tier
achievement, so your quest mastery and energy infrastructure unlock the rarest honors. Once earned,
titles appear on your [profile](/profile) and in the in-game [Titles](/titles) view.

## Available titles

- **Quest Commander** – Reserved for captains who complete 20 quests.
- **Power Mogul** – Earned by storing 10,000 dWatt in your inventory.
- **Wind Architect** – Granted for installing a 500 W wind turbine.
- **Launch Director** – Awarded after recording five successful launches.

Titles update instantly as your quest log or inventory changes, so you can keep the profile page open
while working toward the next badge.
