Titles celebrate long-term milestones in your save file. Each rank is tied to an existing achievement,
so progressing through quests and energy upgrades automatically updates your standing. Titles are
reserved for the most prestigious achievements, keeping the list tighter than the full achievement
catalog. Once earned, titles appear on your [profile](/profile) and in the in-game [Titles](/titles)
view.

## Available titles

- **Mission Commander** – Reserved for explorers who complete twenty quests.
- **Power Reserve** – Honors players who build a 2,000 dWatt safety margin.
- **Grid Stabilizer** – Awarded after a load-tested inverter proves your grid is steady.
- **Launch Director** – Given to rocketeers who establish a dedicated launchpad.
- **Completionist Laureate** – A prestigious honor for earning the Completionist Award.

Titles update instantly as your quest log or inventory changes, so you can keep the profile page open
while working toward the next badge.
