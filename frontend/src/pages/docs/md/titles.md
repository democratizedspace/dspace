Titles celebrate long-term milestones in your save file. Each rank is tied to an existing
achievement, but only the most prestigious achievements grant titles. Once earned, titles appear on
your [profile](/profile) and in the in-game [Titles](/titles) view.

## Available titles

- **Rookie Explorer** – Unlocks when you complete your first quest.
- **Mission Specialist** – Granted after you finish ten quests across any storyline.
- **Campaign Veteran** – Awarded for completing twenty-five quests across any storyline.
- **Grid Steward** – Earned by storing a combined 2,000 dWatt in your inventory.
- **Power Magnate** – Reserved for storing a combined 10,000 dWatt in your inventory.
- **Renewables Integrator** – Celebrates operating both solar and wind power sources.

Titles update instantly as your quest log or inventory changes, so you can keep the profile page
open while working toward the next badge. As more achievements arrive, titles will continue to be
reserved for the most prestigious tiers.
