Titles celebrate long-term milestones in your save file. Each rank is tied to a prestigious
achievement, so progressing through quests and energy upgrades automatically updates your standing.
Once earned, titles appear on your [profile](/profile) and in the in-game [Titles](/titles) view.

## Available titles

- **Quest Vanguard** – Reserved for commanders who finish 30 quests.
- **Grid Magnate** – Honors players who build a 5,000 dWatt reserve.
- **Solar Architect** – Awarded for assembling a full 1 kWh solar setup.
- **Guidance Officer** – Marks the completion of a guided flight stack.
- **Stellar Cartographer** – Recognizes explorers who capture a stacked star trail photo.

Titles update instantly as your quest log or inventory changes, so you can keep the profile page open
while working toward the next badge.
