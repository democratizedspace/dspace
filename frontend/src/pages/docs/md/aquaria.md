Aquaria in DSPACE focuses on maintaining healthy, stable freshwater systems. You will learn
how to balance water chemistry, support aquatic life, and document care routines that scale
from a starter tank to larger habitats.

## What you will practice

- **Water chemistry**: pH balancing, dechlorination, and temperature control.
- **Life support**: filtration, aeration, and lighting schedules.
- **Habitat care**: feeding, plant maintenance, and monitoring fish behavior.
- **Lifecycle management**: breeding guppies and maintaining stable populations.

## Quest flow highlights

Aquaria quests start with lighting installs and pH balance checks, then progress into filter
care, plant additions, and guppy breeding. The focus is on steady parameters and
documentation rather than rapid changes.

## Inventory highlights

- 150 L aquariums, sponge and hang-on-back filters, and aquarium heaters.
- Goldfish and guppies with appropriate food and habitat accessories.
- Floating plants like guppy grass and water chemistry monitoring supplies.

## Why it matters

Stable aquatic systems are foundational for long-term habitation and integrated food
systems. Aquaria teaches you how to keep living environments consistent and resilient.
