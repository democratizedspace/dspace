Aquaria quests focus on freshwater tank setup, water chemistry, and fish care. The line balances
practical maintenance (filters, heaters, water changes) with observation habits that keep aquatic
life healthy.

## What you'll practice

- **Tank setup** — place the aquarium, add substrate, and install heaters and lights.
- **Water chemistry** — test pH, balance parameters, and log results.
- **Filtration and hygiene** — rinse filters, top off evaporation, and remove waste.
- **Animal care** — feed goldfish, guppies, or shrimp and observe behavior changes.

## Inventory and tools to expect

Common aquaria items include:

- 150 L aquariums, heaters, LED lights, and stick-on thermometers
- Sponge filters, nets, gravel, and water conditioners
- Starter fish like goldfish and guppies

## Care checklist

- Dechlorinate water before any top-off or water change.
- Keep temperature steady; sudden swings stress fish.
- Log every test result so you can spot drift early.
