Aquaria skills cover building healthy freshwater habitats, from cycling a tank to logging water
parameters. The quests focus on practical maintenance and repeatable routines.

## What this skill covers

- Setting up tanks, filtration, heaters, and lighting.
- Testing and logging pH, temperature, ammonia, nitrite, and nitrate.
- Performing water changes, top-offs, and filter maintenance.
- Caring for livestock like goldfish, guppies, and shrimp.

## Recommended inventory

- Aquarium tank, filter, heater, thermometer, and LED light.
- Water conditioner, pH test strips, and liquid test kit.
- Gravel vacuum, net, and logbook for maintenance tracking.

## Quest highlights

- [Position the tank](/quests/aquaria/position-tank) and
  [Heater install](/quests/aquaria/heater-install) set the foundation.
- [Water testing](/quests/aquaria/water-testing) and
  [Log water parameters](/quests/aquaria/log-water-parameters) build habits.
- [Water change](/quests/aquaria/water-change) and
  [Filter rinse](/quests/aquaria/filter-rinse) cover upkeep.
- [Walstad tank](/quests/aquaria/walstad) introduces planted, low-tech systems.

## Best practices

1. Dechlorinate all tap water before adding it to the tank.
2. Keep a written log of every test, feed, and water change.
3. Match temperature when topping off or changing water.
4. Quarantine new livestock when possible.
