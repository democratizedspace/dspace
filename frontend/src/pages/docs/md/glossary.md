## DSPACE

Short for Democratized Space (sometimes stylized as democratized.space).

## Guild

A collection of players populating the solar system under the same banner. A guild has its own unique name and 2-4 character tag. Tags consist of any combination of the 26 letters of the English alphabet.

## Hyperstructure

A [hyperstructure](https://jacob.energy/hyperstructures.html) is a set of protocols that adhere to the criteria listed in the [jacob.energy](https://jacob.energy/hyperstructures.html) article.

## Built-in entity

Any quest, item, or process that ships with DSPACE by default. Built-in entities live in the core
catalogs and are available to all players.

## Custom entity

Any player-created quest, item, or process created through the in-game editors. Custom entities can
be exported in content bundles and shared.

## Dialogue node

A single step in a quest conversation. Nodes contain the NPC text shown to the player plus the
options that determine where the quest goes next.

## Entity

The shared term for the game’s editable content types: quests, processes, and items.

## Inventory

The collection of items a player owns. Items in your inventory can be required or consumed by
processes and quest options.

## Item

A collectible resource or tool that can be stored in inventory and used in processes or quests.

## NPC

Non-player character. NPCs are the characters who deliver quest dialogue and guide players through
storylines.

## Process

Given certain required items (and items that are consumed by the [process](/docs/processes), create certain new items). The primary method for building objects, machines, decor, plants, etc.

## Quest

A task that can be completed by the player. It involves branching dialogue with an NPC, a variety of objects either given or received,

## Quest option

A choice the player selects during a quest dialogue node. Options can move to another node, invoke
a process, grant items, or finish the quest.

## Quest tree

Themed collections of quests grouped by topic or learning goal.

## Questline

A series of quests that are related to each other. Questlines are broken down into **quests**.
