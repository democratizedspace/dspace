## DSPACE

Short for Democratized Space (sometimes stylized as democratized.space).

## Entity

The core types of content in DSPACE: quests, processes, and items.

## Built-in entity

An entity shipped with the game by default. Built-in entities are viewable in the UI but are
read-only in creation and editing tools.

## Custom entity

An entity created by players through the in-game tools. Custom entities can be edited and shared,
then submitted for inclusion in the canonical catalog.

## dCarbon

The in-game unit that tracks carbon dioxide generated by player activity. See
[dCarbon](/docs/dCarbon) for details.

## dUSD

The in-game currency used to buy and sell items. See [dUSD](/docs/dusd) for details.

## dWatt

The in-game unit for power generation and consumption in processes. See [dWatt](/docs/dwatt) for
details.

## Guild

A collection of players populating the solar system under the same banner. A guild has its own unique name and 2-4 character tag. Tags consist of any combination of the 26 letters of the English alphabet.

## Hyperstructure

A [hyperstructure](https://jacob.energy/hyperstructures.html) is a set of protocols that adhere to the criteria listed in the [jacob.energy](https://jacob.energy/hyperstructures.html) article.

## Inventory

The list of items a player owns. Inventory counts go up and down as items are earned, consumed, or
traded through quests, processes, and shops.

## Item

An object stored in the inventory, such as materials, machines, currencies, or rewards. Items are
often required as inputs to processes or granted as quest rewards.

## NPC

Non-player character. NPCs guide players through quests and provide dialogue, lore, and
instructions.

## Process

An action that converts one or more items into other items. Processes are the primary way to build
objects, machines, decor, and plants in DSPACE.

## Quest

A task completed by the player, usually with branching dialogue from an NPC and item requirements
or rewards.

## Questline

A series of quests that are related to each other. Questlines are broken down into **quests**.
