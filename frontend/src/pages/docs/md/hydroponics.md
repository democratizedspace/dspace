Hydroponics is the practice of growing plants in nutrient-rich water. In DSPACE the quests focus
on small-batch, repeatable systems that teach you to balance pH, EC, and sanitation so crops
stay healthy in constrained environments.

## What you'll practice

- **System setup** — assemble tubs, pumps, air stones, and net cups.
- **Water chemistry** — measure pH/EC, adjust nutrients, and calibrate meters.
- **Plant care** — germinate basil or lettuce, rinse roots, and harvest safely.
- **Maintenance** — refresh reservoirs, clean filters, and keep algae under control.

## Inventory and tools to expect

Hydroponics inventory includes:

- Starter kits and 20 L tubs with net pots
- Rockwool starter plugs, basil seeds, and nutrient concentrates
- pH strips, EC meters, air stones, and dechlorinated water buckets

## Best practices

- Log every pH and EC adjustment; trends matter more than single readings.
- Flush the root zone when salts build up, then recharge nutrients.
- Use clean tools to avoid algae or biofilm outbreaks.
