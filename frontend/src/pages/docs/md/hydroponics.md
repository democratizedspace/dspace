Hydroponics is the practice of growing plants in nutrient-rich water instead of soil. In DSPACE,
this skill ties together water treatment, sensor checks, and steady crop output.

## What you learn

- How to assemble a hydroponic tub with pumps, net cups, and grow lights
- How to manage pH, EC, and nutrient refresh cycles
- How to sanitize equipment and keep roots healthy

## Key gear and materials

- Hydroponics kits, tubs, net cups, and submersible pumps
- Nutrient concentrate, pH test strips, digital pH meters, and EC meters
- Grow lights, air stones, and dechlorinated water

## Good practices

- Log pH and EC readings every time you top off or refresh the reservoir.
- Rinse and sanitize components between crops to prevent algae and salt buildup.
