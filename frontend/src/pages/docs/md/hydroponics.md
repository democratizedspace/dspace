Hydroponics in DSPACE is a full-cycle farming system: you condition water, tune nutrients,
monitor pH and EC, and harvest crops. It is a core survival skill for off-world habitats
and pairs directly with chemistry and energy systems.

## What you will practice

- **Water prep**: dechlorination, reservoir sanitation, and safe handling.
- **Nutrients**: mixing concentrates, dosing by volume, and logging strength.
- **Plant care**: germination, seedling care, pruning, and harvest timing.
- **Instrumentation**: pH strips, digital meters, and EC calibration.

## Quest flow highlights

Hydroponics quests move from soaking air stones and starting plugs to growing basil and
stevia. Mid-tier quests cover EC calibration, pH checks, and nutrient adjustments. Later
quests focus on harvest quality, regrowth, and keeping systems clean for the next cycle.

## Inventory highlights

- Hydroponics kits, tubs, pumps, tubing, and net cups.
- Nutrient concentrates, pH down solution, and hydrogen peroxide for sanitation.
- pH strips, digital pH meters, EC meters, and calibration solution.
- Grow lights, timers, basil seeds, and harvestable basil plants.

## Why it matters

Hydroponics turns energy and water into food. The same rigor you apply here enables
chemistry extraction quests and provides reliable inputs for long-term habitation.
