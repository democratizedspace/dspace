Hydroponics quests in DSPACE guide you through growing food with clean, controlled water systems.
You will manage nutrients, pH, lighting, and hygiene while tracking plant health in inventory.

## What this skill covers

- Building reservoirs, pumps, and aeration for steady root zones.
- Monitoring pH and EC to keep nutrient solutions in range.
- Sanitizing equipment and preventing algae or pathogen outbreaks.
- Harvesting and logging crops like basil, lettuce, and stevia.

## Recommended inventory

- Hydroponics starter kit, net pots, and a submersible pump.
- Grow lights and timers for consistent photoperiods.
- pH strips, EC meters, and calibration solutions.
- Nutrient concentrate and clean buckets of dechlorinated water.

## Quest highlights

- [Pump install](/quests/hydroponics/pump-install) and
  [Air stone soak](/quests/hydroponics/air-stone-soak) start the system.
- [pH check](/quests/hydroponics/ph-check) and
  [EC check](/quests/hydroponics/ec-check) confirm nutrient balance.
- [Basil](/quests/hydroponics/basil) and [Lettuce](/quests/hydroponics/lettuce) track growth.
- [Reservoir refresh](/quests/hydroponics/reservoir-refresh) closes the maintenance loop.

## Best practices

1. Log readings every time you adjust nutrients or top off water.
2. Sanitize tools and containers between grows to avoid cross-contamination.
3. Use timers for lighting to maintain consistent cycles.
4. Keep a buffer of dechlorinated water ready for quick top-offs.
