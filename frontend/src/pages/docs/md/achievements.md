Achievements celebrate major milestones in your save file. They unlock the first time you meet the
listed requirements and update automatically whenever your quest log or inventory changes. Each
entry in the Achievements menu tracks progress toward its goal so you can see how close you are to
the next badge.

## Current achievements

- **First Steps** – Complete your first quest.
- **Camp Starter** – Acquire your first crafting tool or basic shelter item.
- **Pathfinder** – Finish five quests across any quest line.
- **Mission Specialist** – Finish ten quests across any quest line.
- **Campaign Veteran** – Finish twenty-five quests across any quest line.
- **Storyline Keeper** – Complete an entire quest line from start to finish.
- **Knowledge Seeker** – Read five in-game documentation entries.
- **Inventory Curator** – Collect 25 unique inventory items.
- **Workshop Regular** – Craft or upgrade 10 items.
- **Process Operator** – Run 10 processes successfully.
- **Systems Engineer** – Run 50 processes successfully.
- **Energy Investor** – Store a combined total of 500 dWatt in your inventory.
- **Grid Steward** – Store a combined total of 2,000 dWatt in your inventory.
- **Power Magnate** – Store a combined total of 10,000 dWatt in your inventory.
- **Solar Starter** – Install your first solar panel.
- **Wind Pioneer** – Acquire at least one 500 W wind turbine.
- **Renewables Integrator** – Operate both a solar and wind power source.
- **Hydroponics Steward** – Maintain a thriving hydroponics setup.
- **Workshop Director** – Maintain a production chain of five linked processes.
- **Community Builder** – Unlock five NPC conversations or dialogue branches.

Visit [/achievements](/achievements) to view your progress. Cards update in real time as you finish
quests, generate dWatt, or pick up new equipment. Progress is stored locally alongside your game
state, so achievements persist between sessions without sending any data to a server.
