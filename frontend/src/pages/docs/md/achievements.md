Achievements celebrate major milestones in your save file. They unlock the first time you meet the
listed requirements and update automatically whenever your quest log or inventory changes. Each
entry in the Achievements menu tracks progress toward its goal so you can see how close you are to
the next badge.

## Current achievements

### Quests

- **First Steps** – Complete your first quest.
- **Trailblazer** – Finish five quests across any storyline.
- **Mission Specialist** – Finish 10 quests to prove your dedication.
- **Quest Commander** – Complete 20 quests and lead the expedition.

### Energy

- **Charge Collector** – Store 100 dWatt in your inventory.
- **Energy Investor** – Store 500 dWatt across your inventory.
- **Power Reserve** – Build a 2,000 dWatt emergency reserve.
- **Solar Scout** – Install a portable solar panel.
- **Battery Steward** – Secure a 200 Wh battery pack for storage.
- **Field Ready** – Assemble a wired portable solar kit.
- **Wind Pioneer** – Add a 500 W wind turbine to your base.
- **AC Uplink** – Mount a 300 W inverter safely.
- **Grid Stable** – Load-test an inverter and confirm stable output.

### Fabrication

- **Printer Online** – Bring an entry-level FDM 3D printer online.
- **Calibration Captain** – Print a Benchy calibration model.

### Rocketry

- **Rocket Crafter** – Print your first model rocket airframe.
- **Pad Builder** – Set up a dedicated model rocket launchpad.

### Hydroponics

- **Green Initiate** – Establish your first hydroponics kit.
- **Nutrient Steward** – Stock 1 L of hydroponic nutrient concentrate.

### Awards

- **Completionist** – Earn the Completionist Award trophy.

Visit [/achievements](/achievements) to view your progress. Cards update in real time as you finish
quests, generate dWatt, or pick up new equipment. Progress is stored locally alongside your game
state, so achievements persist between sessions without sending any data to a server.
