Achievements celebrate major milestones in your save file. They unlock the first time you meet the
listed requirements and update automatically whenever your quest log or inventory changes. Each
entry in the Achievements menu tracks progress toward its goal so you can see how close you are to
the next badge.

## Current achievements

### Quests

- **First Steps** – Complete your first quest.
- **Quest Scout** – Finish 5 quests across any quest line.
- **Mission Specialist** – Finish 10 quests to prove your dedication.
- **Quest Commander** – Complete 20 quests across the map.

### Energy

- **Energy Investor** – Store 500 dWatt across your inventory.
- **Power Banker** – Bank 2,000 dWatt in storage.
- **Power Mogul** – Amass 10,000 dWatt in storage.
- **Carbon Counter** – Collect 100 dCarbon.
- **Offset Advocate** – Earn 25 dOffset through carbon reductions.
- **Solar Starter** – Add a portable solar panel to your gear.
- **Solar Operator** – Wire up a portable solar kit.
- **Wind Pioneer** – Add a 500 W wind turbine to your base.

### Rocketry

- **Print the Rocket** – Build a 3D printed model rocket.
- **Launch Director** – Set up a model rocket launchpad.
- **Flight Ready** – Assemble a launch-capable model rocket.
- **Flight Log** – Record 5 successful launches.

### Hydroponics

- **Hydroponics Starter** – Acquire a hydroponics kit.
- **Photosynthesis Crew** – Install a hydroponic grow lamp.
- **Basil Harvest** – Collect a bundle of basil leaves.

### Aquarium

- **Aquarium Keeper** – Set up your first 150 L aquarium.

Visit [/achievements](/achievements) to view your progress. Cards update in real time as you finish
quests, generate dWatt, or pick up new equipment. Progress is stored locally alongside your game
state, so achievements persist between sessions without sending any data to a server.
