Electronics quests cover sensing, soldering, and measurement skills that power robotics, energy
systems, and data logging.

## What you learn

- How to build LED circuits and voltage dividers
- How to read sensors like thermistors and photoresistors
- How to solder, desolder, and validate continuity with a multimeter

## Quest trailheads

- [Build a basic LED circuit](/quests/electronics/basic-circuit)
- [Check LED polarity with a multimeter](/quests/electronics/led-polarity)
- [Measure a Resistor](/quests/electronics/measure-resistance)
- [Solder a Wire Connection](/quests/electronics/solder-wire)
- [Read a Thermistor](/quests/electronics/thermistor-reading)
- [Build a Light Sensor](/quests/electronics/light-sensor)

## Key gear and supplies

- Digital multimeter and GFCI outlet tester
- Soldering iron kit, solder wick, desoldering pump, and flux pen
- Wire strippers, cutters, and heat-shrink tubing

## Good practices

- Always verify power is off before soldering or rewiring a circuit.
- Label your test leads and keep a small log of sensor calibration values.
