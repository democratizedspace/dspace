Electronics quests teach circuit fundamentals, soldering, and sensor work. You will build small
test circuits, measure signals, and document results for repeatable builds.

## What this skill covers

- Breadboard fundamentals, polarity checks, and component identification.
- Soldering, desoldering, and wire prep best practices.
- Measuring voltage, current, resistance, and continuity.
- Basic microcontroller or sensor workflows.

## Recommended inventory

- Soldering iron kit, solder, flux pen, and fume extractor.
- Multimeter, wire strippers, and precision screwdrivers.
- Resistors, thermistors, LEDs, and jumper wire for starter circuits.

## Quest highlights

- [Soldering intro](/quests/electronics/soldering-intro) and
  [Solder wire](/quests/electronics/solder-wire) build fundamentals.
- [Measure resistance](/quests/electronics/measure-resistance) and
  [Continuity test](/quests/electronics/continuity-test) validate circuits.
- [Thermistor reading](/quests/electronics/thermistor-reading) introduces sensors.
- [Arduino blink](/quests/electronics/arduino-blink) is the first microcontroller win.

## Best practices

1. Work on a clean, static-safe surface with good ventilation.
2. Tin tips and wires before final joints for consistent solder flow.
3. Label wires and keep wiring diagrams in your logbook.
4. Verify polarity before powering circuits.
