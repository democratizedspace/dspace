---
title: 'dOffset'
slug: 'doffset'
---

dOffset represents verified carbon offsets in DSPACE. It is minted by retiring dCarbon with a
small amount of dUSD, giving you a way to balance emissions created when you generate dWatt
from non-renewable sources.

You earn dOffset by running the **dCarbon to dOffset** process in the **Processes** tab. Each
run consumes dCarbon, pays the retirement fee in dUSD, and credits dOffset back to your
inventory.

dOffset is closely tied to dWatt and dCarbon:

- Generating dWatt from wall outlets accrues dCarbon.
- Converting dCarbon into dOffset lets you clean up that footprint and keep your emissions
  totals manageable.
