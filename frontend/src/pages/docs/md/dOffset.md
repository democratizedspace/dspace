dOffset represents verified emissions offsets in the game. Each dOffset token stands for retiring
1 kg of carbon dioxide that was previously tracked as [dCarbon](/docs/dCarbon).

## How dOffset works

- Accumulate dCarbon when you mint [dWatt](/docs/dwatt) from fossil-fuel sources like an outlet.
- Run the **dCarbon to dOffset** process to retire emissions and convert dCarbon into dOffset.
- dOffset lets you balance your energy footprint while still using dWatt to power processes.

To avoid needing offsets in the first place, generate dWatt from renewable sources like solar or
wind, which do not accrue dCarbon.
