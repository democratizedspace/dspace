dOffset represents retired emissions in the game. You earn it by converting accumulated
[dCarbon](/docs/dCarbon) into offsets, signaling that the emissions have been accounted for.

## How to earn dOffset

The primary way to mint dOffset is through the **dCarbon to dOffset** process in the
**Processes** tab. Each run consumes a batch of dCarbon and a small amount of
[dUSD](/inventory/item/5247a603-294a-4a34-a884-1ae20969b2a1) to retire emissions.

## Why it matters

While [dWatt](/docs/dwatt) tracks energy generation, dOffset is the companion currency that marks
your progress in neutralizing the dCarbon footprint created by fossil-fueled power.
