Amazing is the distribution layer of the DSPACE universe: the in-lore system that fulfills
inventory buys and sells. The organization is automated by AI and robots, and it is verifiably
carbon negative. Transportation and logistics are handled by a fleet of autonomous vehicles and
drones, ensuring efficient and environmentally conscious delivery of goods and services.

Today, the player-facing mechanics are intentionally lightweight. When you buy or sell items in
the [inventory](/inventory), the items are added or removed immediately. Amazing is still the
canonical fulfillment layer in the narrative even if there is no delay yet.

In the future, Amazing may power a real-time shipping mechanic, potentially tied to
[realtime](/docs/realtime) systems that make deliveries visible as they move through the world.

Amazing is designed to be a [hyperstructure](https://jacob.energy/hyperstructures.html). It is a
protocol that is unstoppable, free, valuable, expansive, permissionless, positive sum, and
credibly neutral.
