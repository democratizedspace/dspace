## What can I do?

There are a variety of quests to complete. Quests are the primary way to learn about the lore and
mechanics of the game. If you're all caught up, try building your own adventure with the
[custom quest system](/docs/custom-quest-system) or replay story arcs to see the alternate
outcomes hidden behind optional objectives.

## What is DSPACE?

DSPACE is a world-building game about cooperative systems design. The [About](/docs/about) and
[Mission](/docs/mission) pages provide the broader vision, while the [Roadmap](/docs/roadmap)
shares what we are building next.

## How do quests work?

[Quest guidelines](/docs/quest-guidelines) explain the structure of quest nodes, objectives, and
outcomes. If you want to follow larger arcs, [quest trees](/docs/quest-trees) map how quests link
together across a storyline.

## How do I submit custom quests?

Use the [Quest Submission Guide](/docs/quest-submission) to package your quest, process, or item
so it can be reviewed for inclusion in the canon game. The guide walks through exporting your
content, generating screenshots, and opening a pull request directly from the in-game tools.

## What if I want to contribute more than a quest?

Start with [Contribute](/docs/contribute) for ways to help. The
[Contributor's Guide](/docs/contributors-guide) covers expectations, while
[Content Development](/docs/content-development) outlines how quests, items, and processes are
reviewed. For deeper technical detail, use the [Quest Schema](/docs/quest-schema) and
[Quest Template](/docs/quest-template) references.

## How do I make progress?

[Quests](/docs/quest-guidelines) are the primary way to make progress in the game. They can grant
certain items and introduce you to [processes](/docs/processes).

## What are processes and how do they work?

[Processes](/docs/processes) are something you can do to convert one or more items into other
items. This is the primary way to create items in the game.

## How do inventory items work?

The [Inventory](/docs/inventory) guide explains how items are stored and used. If you're
contributing new items, follow the [Item Guidelines](/docs/item-guidelines) to keep balance and
metadata consistent.

## Where can I learn about crafting or production?

[Process guidelines](/docs/process-guidelines) document how to design balanced crafting flows, and
the [Processes](/docs/processes) reference lists what is currently available.

## How do achievements and titles work?

Achievements document milestones and are summarized in the [Achievements](/docs/achievements)
guide. Some achievements unlock [Titles](/docs/titles) that show up in player profiles.

## What are guilds?

[Guilds](/docs/guilds) are player groups that coordinate quests, production, and shared goals.

## How do I find newly added quests?

The [New Quests](/docs/new-quests) page highlights recent additions and helps you spot what to
play next.

## Can I search the documentation?

Yes. The [Docs Search](/docs/docs-search) page explains search tips and query syntax.

## Where can I track changes to the game?

Use the [Changelog](/docs/changelog) for release notes and the [Outages](/docs/outages) log for
incident history and status updates.

## How do I keep my save data safe?

Review [Cloud Sync](/docs/cloud-sync) and [Backups](/docs/backups) for recommended protections.
[Rollback](/docs/rollback) and [Legacy Save Storage](/docs/legacy-save-storage) explain recovery
options if something goes wrong.

## Can I run DSPACE myself?

Yes. The [Self Hosting](/docs/self-hosting) guide covers requirements and setup for local or
community deployments.

## What are dUSD, dCarbon, and dWatt?

These are in-world resource tokens. See [dUSD](/docs/dusd), [dCarbon](/docs/dCarbon), and
[dWatt](/docs/dwatt) for details on how they tie into quests and processes.

## Where do I learn about core world systems like power or manufacturing?

Explore [Electricity](/docs/electricity), [Solar](/docs/solar), [Hydroponics](/docs/hydroponics),
[Chemistry Lab](/docs/chemistry-lab), and [3D Printing](/docs/3dprinting) for the main production
loops.

## Where can I learn more?

On the [Discord](https://discord.gg/A3UAfYvnxM) or here in the [docs](/docs).
