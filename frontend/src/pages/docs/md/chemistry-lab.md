DSPACE's chemistry skill tree is a practical lab track: learn safe handling, accurate
measurement, and reproducible processes that connect hydroponics outputs to useful
compounds.

## Core tools

- **Hot plate** for controlled heating and evaporation.
- **Beakers, funnels, and filter paper** for filtration and transfer.
- **Scale and measuring syringes** for precise dosing.
- **Thermometer and pH tools** for monitoring reactions.
- **Spill tray and PPE** (gloves, eye protection) for safety.

## Safety essentials

1. Use eye protection and gloves when heating or mixing.
2. Work in a well-ventilated area away from flames.
3. Label all containers and neutralize acids before disposal.
4. Keep a first aid kit and spill supplies within reach.

## Quest flow highlights

Chemistry quests start with safe dilutions, acid spill response, and buffer preparation.
You then move into pH measurement and adjustment before extracting and refining stevia
crystals from hydroponics harvests. The loop closes with taste testing and documentation.

## Inventory highlights

- Lab glassware, funnels, filter paper, and spill trays.
- pH strips, meters, and calibration solutions.
- Hydrogen peroxide and neutralization supplies.
- Stevia-related ingredients and storage containers.

## Why it matters

Chemistry makes raw biomass more valuable. By keeping the lab safe and repeatable, you can
create consistent extracts, sweeteners, and inputs for future biotech quests.
