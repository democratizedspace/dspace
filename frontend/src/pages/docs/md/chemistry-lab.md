Chemistry lab basics focus on safe handling, measurement, and documentation. DSPACE chemistry
quests emphasize pH, solutions, and careful record keeping rather than high-risk reactions.

## What this skill covers

- Safe handling of acids, bases, and reagents with proper PPE.
- Measuring and diluting solutions accurately.
- Recording test results and creating repeatable procedures.
- Applying chemistry to hydroponics, water quality, and materials.

## Recommended inventory

- Glassware (beakers, graduated cylinders, stir rods) and a spill tray.
- Safety gear: goggles, lab coat, nitrile gloves, and a first aid kit.
- Common reagents like hydrochloric acid and pH adjustment solutions.

## Quest highlights

- [pH test](/quests/chemistry/ph-test) and
  [pH adjustment](/quests/chemistry/ph-adjustment) cover basic measurement and correction.
- [Acid dilution](/quests/chemistry/acid-dilution) reinforces safe mixing order.
- [Buffer solution](/quests/chemistry/buffer-solution) introduces repeatable references.
- [Stevia extraction](/quests/chemistry/stevia-extraction) brings lab work into food systems.

## Best practices

1. Always add acid to water, never the other way around.
2. Label containers with concentration, date, and handler initials.
3. Keep a clean spill tray and neutralization materials nearby.
4. Wash hands and surfaces after every session.
