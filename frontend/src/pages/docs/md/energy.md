Energy systems in DSPACE are about turning renewable generation into reliable, stored power.
This track connects solar, wind, batteries, and inverters into a usable off-grid stack.

## What you will practice

- **Generation mix**: pairing solar with wind for round-the-clock output.
- **Storage design**: sizing battery packs and tracking charge cycles.
- **Load planning**: matching inverters and appliances to available capacity.
- **Operational logging**: documenting charge controller settings and load tests.

## Quest flow highlights

Energy quests include configuring charge controllers, installing larger batteries, and
accumulating dSolar. Additional tasks focus on biogas digester builds and long-term
maintenance of power systems.

## Inventory highlights

- Portable solar kits, wind turbines, charge controllers, and fused leads.
- 200 Wh and 1 kWh LiFePO4 battery packs plus inverter setups.
- dSolar, dWind, and dWatt tokens for tracking energy sources.

## Why it matters

Sustainable energy keeps everything else running. A stable energy system supports
hydroponics, chemistry labs, robotics, and the compute stack.
