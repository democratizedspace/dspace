The UBI skill track teaches the fundamentals of DSPACE's economy: claiming income, planning
savings, and setting reminders so you never miss a payout window.

## What you will practice

- **Claiming income**: logging in and redeeming your daily or periodic UBI.
- **Budgeting**: setting up a savings jar and tracking balances.
- **Habits**: scheduling reminders so payouts are never missed.

## Quest flow highlights

UBI quests include claiming your first UBI, setting reminders, and building a savings jar to
keep long-term goals visible.

## Inventory highlights

- UBI savings jars and journal entries.
- Budgeting logs that track inflows and targets.

## Why it matters

UBI keeps the economy accessible and rewards consistent participation. It provides the
baseline resources needed to invest in tools, energy, and learning.
