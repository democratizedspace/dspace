# Stats

`/stats` is the quick health panel for your save. It gives a live summary of progression without
opening every subsystem page.

## What you can verify on the Stats page

- Total quests and completion progress
- Inventory breadth/collection growth
- Process activity totals

## Why it matters in v3

With larger quest trees and custom content tooling, Stats is the fastest way to confirm:

- a migration/import did not lose progress,
- a content test run changed expected totals,
- a new save bootstrap is behaving as expected.
