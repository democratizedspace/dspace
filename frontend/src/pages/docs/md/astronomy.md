Astronomy quests teach observation, tracking, and basic astrophotography. You will learn to
identify targets, document sightings, and build a simple telescope using accessible materials.

## What this skill covers

- Identifying constellations, planets, and deep-sky objects.
- Tracking events like ISS flyovers, meteor showers, and eclipses.
- Building and aligning a basic telescope.
- Capturing and logging observations, sketches, and photos.

## Recommended inventory

- Magnifying lenses and a cardboard tube for a basic telescope build.
- Tripod or stable mount for binoculars or cameras.
- Logbook for recording dates, sky conditions, and targets.

## Quest highlights

- [Basic telescope](/quests/astronomy/basic-telescope) builds a starter instrument.
- [Locate the North Star](/quests/astronomy/north-star) trains navigation by sky.
- [ISS flyover](/quests/astronomy/iss-flyover) adds timing and tracking skills.
- [Star trails](/quests/astronomy/star-trails) introduces long-exposure capture.

## Best practices

1. Check local light pollution and weather before scheduling a session.
2. Keep optics clean and avoid touching lenses with bare fingers.
3. Log time, location, and sky conditions for every observation.
4. Warm clothing and red light headlamps keep night sessions comfortable.
