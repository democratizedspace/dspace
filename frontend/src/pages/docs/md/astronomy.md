Astronomy in DSPACE is about observation discipline: learning the night sky, building basic
optics, and capturing long-exposure data for documentation.

## What you will practice

- **Sky navigation**: star hops, constellation identification, and ISS pass tracking.
- **Optics basics**: assembling simple telescopes and aligning lenses.
- **Observation logs**: sketches, window cards, and photo stacking.
- **Seasonal planning**: timing observations for comets, aurora, and planets.

## Quest flow highlights

Astronomy quests cover assembling a simple telescope, tracking the ISS, observing planets,
locating galaxies, and photographing star trails. Each quest emphasizes patient observation
and clean documentation.

## Inventory highlights

- Basic refracting telescopes, planisphere star charts, and red-light flashlights.
- Telescope optics components and cardboard tube builds.
- Tripod-mounted camera rigs and star trail stacking guides.

## Why it matters

Observation builds situational awareness for off-world navigation and scientific reporting.
Astronomy also feeds into data-driven quests and communications disciplines.
