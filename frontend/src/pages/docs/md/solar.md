Solar power in DSPACE emphasizes portable, safe, and measurable energy generation. You will wire
small systems, log output, and understand how solar production connects to dSolar and dWatt.

## What this skill covers

- Selecting panels, orienting arrays, and tracking output over time.
- Wiring charge controllers, batteries, and inverters safely.
- Measuring energy production and documenting system health.
- Scaling from portable panels to larger renewable setups.

## Recommended inventory

- Portable solar panel and charge controller kit for field practice.
- Battery packs, fused cable kits, and a pure sine inverter for loads.
- Smart plugs or power strips to measure consumption downstream.

## Best practices

1. Always fuse close to the battery to protect wiring.
2. Record panel angle, weather, and output for each run.
3. Avoid shading even small parts of a panel; it reduces array output.
4. Verify polarity before connecting controllers or inverters.
