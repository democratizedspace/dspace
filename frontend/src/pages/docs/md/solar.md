Renewable power in DSPACE covers the full off-grid stack: generation, storage, and safe delivery
of electricity to the tools you run. Solar is the first on-ramp, but the questline expands into
wind, biogas, batteries, and geothermal monitoring.

## What you'll practice

- **Generation** — deploy portable solar panels, stand up wind turbines, or prep a biogas digester.
- **Storage** — size and maintain LiFePO4 batteries so your load stays stable after sunset.
- **Power delivery** — wire charge controllers, inverters, and load tests to move energy safely.
- **Site awareness** — log ground temperature deltas for geothermal scouting.

## Inventory and tools to expect

Energy gear in the inventory includes:

- Portable solar panels and wired solar kits
- 200 Wh LiFePO4 battery packs and fused cable kits
- Pure sine inverters for safe AC loads

## Sustainability notes

- Pair solar and wind quests to keep generation steady through day/night cycles.
- Always fuse battery leads and verify polarity before energizing the inverter.
- Log charge cycles; it keeps the dWatt economy honest and your hardware healthy.
