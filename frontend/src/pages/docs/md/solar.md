Solar power is the backbone of DSPACE's renewable energy stack. The inventory now includes
portable 200 W panels, wired solar kits, charge controllers, and weatherproof enclosures so
you can go from a folding panel to a permanent off-grid setup.

## What you will practice

- **System sizing**: matching panel output to battery storage and expected loads.
- **Safe wiring**: fused DC leads, strain relief, and controller configuration.
- **Verification**: load-testing inverters and logging real-world charging performance.
- **Energy accounting**: earning dSolar and converting it into usable dWatt power.

## Quest flow highlights

The energy questline covers configuring solar charge controllers, wiring portable kits, and
scaling up to larger battery packs. Later quests focus on accumulating dSolar and validating
longer-term energy output from solar arrays.

## Inventory highlights

- Foldable 200 W portable panels, wired solar kits, and 30 A charge controllers.
- 200 Wh and 1 kWh LiFePO4 battery packs for storage.
- 300 W pure sine inverters, fused leads, and weatherproof solar enclosures.
- dSolar and dWatt tokens for tracking generation and consumption.

## Why it matters

Solar is the most approachable renewable in DSPACE. It unlocks clean electricity that keeps
your systems running, reduces dCarbon penalties, and pairs well with wind turbines and
storage to deliver round-the-clock power.
