Solar power is a renewable energy source that has a number of benefits:

-   Solar panels don't require moving parts (in most cases)
-   Solar panels can be added incrementally
-   Solar panels can be made with common materials

Because solar arrays are easy to scale without complex maintenance, they were the first major
renewable energy system shipped in DSPACE. Wind power now complements solar installations: the
[Install a Wind Turbine](/quests/energy/wind-turbine) quest walks you through deploying a 500 W
wind turbine and wiring it into your base so you can generate dWatt even on overcast days. The
[500 W wind turbine](/inventory/item/743681a7-d2e7-465c-af07-43665079bf4d) lives in your inventory
alongside solar hardware and feeds processes like [Charge a 200 Wh battery pack with a 500 W wind
turbine](/processes/base/charge-a-200-wh-battery-pack-with-a-500-w-wind-turbine) without adding
any dCarbon.

Pair solar panels with wind turbines to keep power flowing overnight and during storms, and expand
battery capacity so you can buffer the extra energy for high-demand manufacturing or launch prep.
