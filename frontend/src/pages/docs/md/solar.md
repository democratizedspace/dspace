Solar power is DSPACE's first major renewable skill: it feeds your batteries, mints dSolar, and
reduces your dCarbon footprint.

## What you learn

- How to position, wire, and monitor solar panels for reliable output
- How to scale from a single panel to portable and tracked arrays
- How solar integrates with batteries, charge controllers, and dWatt storage

## Quest trailheads

- [Set up a solar panel](/quests/energy/solar)

- [Build a Solar Tracker](/quests/energy/solar-tracker)
- [Upgrade your solar enclosure with more capacity](/quests/energy/solar-1kWh)
- [Accrue 1,000 dSolar](/quests/energy/dSolar-1kW)

## Key gear and materials

- Portable solar panel and wired solar kit components
- Solar tracker control kit (microcontroller + dual stepper drivers)
- Aluminum extrusion tracker frame hardware for stable one-axis motion
- Charge controller settings and fused cabling for safe hookups
- Battery packs to capture dWatt when the sun is strong

## Solar tracker process notes

- **Build a Solar Tracker** now includes an assembly process that consumes a 200 Wh solar setup,
  a tracker control kit, a stepper motor pair, and an aluminum extrusion frame kit.
- Completing the assembly process creates a new inventory item: **Solar tracking unit (200 Wh)**,
  which the quest then uses for the tracking and verification steps.

## Tips

- Log your panel orientation and weather notes; steady output matters more than peak spikes.
- Wind turbines and storage pair well with solar so nighttime usage doesn't incur dCarbon penalties.
