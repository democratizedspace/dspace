Robotics quests focus on sensing, actuation, and navigation. You will build small robots that can
scan the environment, follow lines, and log movement data.

## What you'll practice

- **Actuation** — drive servos, grippers, and pan-tilt brackets.
- **Sensing** — map obstacles with ultrasonic rangefinders and reflectance sensors.
- **Navigation** — follow lines, measure wheel encoders, and tune odometry.

## Quest path highlights

- Motion basics: [Servo control](/quests/robotics/servo-control),
  [Servo arm](/quests/robotics/servo-arm),
  [Pan-tilt](/quests/robotics/pan-tilt)
- Sensing: [Ultrasonic rangefinder](/quests/robotics/ultrasonic-rangefinder),
  [Reflectance sensors](/quests/robotics/reflectance-sensors)
- Navigation: [Line follower](/quests/robotics/line-follower),
  [Odometry basics](/quests/robotics/odometry-basics),
  [Maze navigation](/quests/robotics/maze-navigation)

## Inventory and tools to expect

Robotics inventory includes:

- Micro servos and servo test rigs
- Ultrasonic distance sensors and pan-tilt brackets
- Wheel encoder kits for odometry work

## Build notes

- Calibrate every servo before you load it with a payload.
- Log sensor sweeps so you can compare against navigation outcomes.
