Robotics in DSPACE combines electronics, programming, and mechanical assembly. The quests
walk you through sensing, navigation, and motion control for small autonomous builds.

## What you will practice

- **Sensors**: ultrasonic ranging, wheel encoders, and basic calibration.
- **Control**: line following, maze navigation, and obstacle avoidance.
- **Mechanics**: assembling servo arms and pan-tilt rigs.
- **Testing**: logging behavior and iterating on movement algorithms.

## Quest flow highlights

Robotics quests cover line-following bots, maze navigation, obstacle avoidance, and gyro
balance. Advanced tasks add distance tracking with wheel encoders and tuning sensor rigs.

## Inventory highlights

- Ultrasonic sensors, micro servos, and pan-tilt brackets.
- Servo arm kits and calibrated motion assemblies.
- Arduino-controlled test rigs for calibration.

## Why it matters

Robotics turns energy and code into physical action. These skills unlock automation for
monitoring, logistics, and exploration quests.
