First aid in DSPACE is about readiness and safe lab practice. The quests cover assembling
kits, maintaining supplies, and documenting safe disposal of used materials.

## What you will practice

- **Preparedness**: assembling a complete kit with bandages, gauze, and antiseptics.
- **Response**: cleaning and dressing minor wounds and replacing used supplies.
- **Hygiene**: bagging contaminated waste and protecting yourself with gloves.
- **Maintenance**: checking flashlight batteries and replacing expired items.

## Quest flow highlights

First aid quests guide you through building a kit, changing bandages, bagging used supplies,
checking flashlights, and disposing of expired materials.

## Inventory highlights

- First aid kits, adhesive bandages, sterile gauze pads, and antiseptic wipes.
- Nitrile gloves and red disposal bags for contaminated waste.
- Emergency flashlights and spare batteries.

## Why it matters

Safety enables every other skill. Proper first aid keeps labs, workshops, and field
operations resilient and responsible.
