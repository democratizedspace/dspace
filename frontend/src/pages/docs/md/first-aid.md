First aid skills keep crews safe and ready. DSPACE quests teach basic wound care, CPR prep, and
kit maintenance so you can respond quickly to common injuries.

## What this skill covers

- Building and maintaining a complete first aid kit.
- Cleaning wounds, applying dressings, and monitoring healing.
- Handling burns, splints, nosebleeds, and minor injuries.
- Safe disposal of used or expired supplies.

## Recommended inventory

- Adhesive bandages, sterile gauze, antiseptic wipes, and antibiotic ointment.
- Nitrile gloves, safety goggles, and a biohazard waste bag.
- CPR pocket mask or training gear for practice sessions.

## Quest highlights

- [Assemble kit](/quests/firstaid/assemble-kit) creates your baseline loadout.
- [Wound care](/quests/firstaid/wound-care) and
  [Change bandage](/quests/firstaid/change-bandage) cover daily hygiene.
- [Treat burn](/quests/firstaid/treat-burn) and
  [Splint limb](/quests/firstaid/splint-limb) handle common incidents.
- [Dispose expired](/quests/firstaid/dispose-expired) keeps supplies fresh.

## Best practices

1. Restock immediately after using supplies.
2. Wash hands and wear gloves before treating wounds.
3. Document treatments in a log for follow-up care.
4. Know when to escalate to professional medical help.
