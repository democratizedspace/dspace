First aid quests focus on preparedness and calm response: assembling kits, treating minor injuries,
and maintaining clean supplies.

## What you learn

- How to stock and restock a basic first aid kit
- How to clean wounds, dress cuts, and manage minor burns
- How to practice CPR techniques with proper hygiene

## Quest trailheads

- [Assemble a First Aid Kit](/quests/firstaid/assemble-kit)
- [Practice Basic Wound Care](/quests/firstaid/wound-care)
- [Restock Your First Aid Kit](/quests/firstaid/restock-kit)
- [Treat a Minor Burn](/quests/firstaid/treat-burn)
- [Stop a Nosebleed](/quests/firstaid/stop-nosebleed)
- [Practice Basic CPR](/quests/firstaid/learn-cpr)

## Key gear and supplies

- Adhesive bandages, sterile gauze, antiseptic wipes, and antibiotic ointment
- Nitrile gloves, safety goggles, and biohazard waste bags
- CPR training gear and a clean work surface

## Good practices

- Replace expired supplies on a schedule and log disposal.
- Wash hands and sanitize surfaces before treating wounds.
