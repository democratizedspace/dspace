First aid quests build readiness for everyday injuries and emergencies. The focus is on clean
procedures, calm decision-making, and keeping supplies stocked.

## What you'll practice

- **Preparedness** — assemble and restock kits, replace expired supplies.
- **Wound care** — clean minor cuts, stop bleeding, and change bandages safely.
- **Injury response** — treat burns, splint limbs, and remove splinters.
- **CPR readiness** — practice with a pocket mask and training manikin.

## Inventory and tools to expect

First aid inventory includes:

- A stocked first aid kit with bandages and gauze
- CPR pocket masks and training manikins
- Nitrile gloves and disposal bags for contaminated waste

## Safety reminder

If a condition worsens or you are unsure, seek professional medical help immediately.
