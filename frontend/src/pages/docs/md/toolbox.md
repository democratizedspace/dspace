# Toolbox

Toolbox (`/toolbox`) is the utilities hub introduced in v3. It consolidates operational pages that
used to be scattered or placeholder-only.

## Typical destinations from Toolbox

- Save management and recovery tools
- Custom content backup/import/export utilities
- Content editors and submission entry points
- Diagnostics and QA-oriented workflows

Use Toolbox when you are maintaining your save, preparing submissions, or troubleshooting state.
