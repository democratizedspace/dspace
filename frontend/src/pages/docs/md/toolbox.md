# Toolbox

`/toolbox` is the utilities hub for backup, content-authoring, and diagnostics surfaces.

## Sections

### Save management

- `/gamesaves`
- `/cloudsync`
- `/contentbackup`

### Custom content editors

- `/inventory/manage`
- `/processes/manage`
- `/quests/manage`
- `/quests/submit`

### Diagnostics and insights

- `/stats`
- `/leaderboard`
- `/docs/changelog`

## Why use Toolbox?

Use Toolbox as the fastest launchpad for operational tasks that are spread across multiple menu
areas. It is especially useful during playtesting, content contribution, and migration rehearsals.
