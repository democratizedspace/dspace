Composting in DSPACE is a systems skill for turning organic waste into soil amendments.
You will learn to manage temperature, moisture, and airflow so compost stays active and
finishes clean.

## What you will practice

- **Carbon-to-nitrogen balance**: layering greens and browns in the right ratios.
- **Moisture control**: keeping compost damp like a wrung sponge.
- **Aeration**: turning buckets and preventing anaerobic conditions.
- **Finishing**: cooling, curing, and screening compost for reuse.

## Quest flow highlights

Composting quests include starting a bucket, monitoring temperature, turning the pile, and
sifting finished compost. The loop closes with documentation logs for moisture and heat.

## Inventory highlights

- Aerated 5-gallon compost buckets, temperature and moisture logs.
- Screened compost blends for top-dressing or seed starts.

## Why it matters

Closed-loop systems are essential for long-term sustainability. Composting feeds into
hydroponics and woodworking by reducing waste and improving growing media.
