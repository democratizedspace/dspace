Composting quests teach you how to turn organic waste into usable soil amendments. The focus is
on temperature, moisture, and aeration so you can run compact bins with predictable outputs.

## What you'll practice

- **Pile setup** — build a layered compost bucket and track carbon/nitrogen balance.
- **Monitoring** — log temperature and moisture to keep microbes active.
- **Turning** — aerate the pile to avoid anaerobic smells.
- **Finishing** — sift and cure compost for safe use.

## Quest path highlights

- Start a pile: [Compost kickoff](/quests/composting/start)
- Monitor: [Check temperature](/quests/composting/check-temperature)
- Aerate: [Turn pile](/quests/composting/turn-pile)
- Finish: [Sift compost](/quests/composting/sift-compost)

## Inventory and tools to expect

Composting inventory includes:

- Compost thermometers and moisture meters
- Sifters and cured compost buckets
- Logs for temperature and moisture checks

## Best practices

- Keep moisture at the “wrung-out sponge” level.
- Turn more often when the core temperature stalls.
