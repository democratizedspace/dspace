Composting quests focus on turning organic waste into a stable soil amendment. You will learn to
balance carbon and nitrogen, monitor heat and moisture, and screen finished compost.

## What this skill covers

- Building and layering a compost pile or bucket system.
- Monitoring temperature and moisture for healthy microbial activity.
- Turning, aerating, and curing material to finish the cycle.
- Screening compost for clean, usable output.

## Recommended inventory

- Compost thermometer and moisture meter for regular checks.
- Bucket or bin with airflow holes for a contained pile.
- Compost sifter for the finishing step.

## Quest highlights

- [Start compost](/quests/composting/start) sets the initial mix.
- [Check temperature](/quests/composting/check-temperature) confirms activity.
- [Turn pile](/quests/composting/turn-pile) re-oxygenates the system.
- [Sift compost](/quests/composting/sift-compost) delivers finished material.

## Best practices

1. Aim for a damp, wrung-sponge texture to keep microbes active.
2. Alternate greens and browns to avoid odors and slowdowns.
3. Record temperature spikes and cooldowns to time turns.
4. Let compost cure before using it in sensitive plantings.

## Where to go next

Use finished compost in woodworking planters or hydroponic seedlings that need a soil boost.
