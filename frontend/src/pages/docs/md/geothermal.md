Geothermal quests focus on monitoring ground loops, heat pumps, and sensor systems. You will learn
how to log temperatures, check pressure, and keep geothermal loops operating efficiently.

## What this skill covers

- Surveying ground temperature by depth and season.
- Installing and calibrating thermistors and backup sensors.
- Monitoring loop pressure, inlet/outlet temperatures, and temperature delta.
- Logging heat pump warmup behavior and energy usage.

## Recommended inventory

- Thermistors, resistors, and a multimeter for sensor circuits.
- Temperature logs or dashboards for long-term monitoring.
- Pump and loop maintenance tools for inspections.

## Quest highlights

- [Survey ground temperature](/quests/geothermal/survey-ground-temperature) sets the baseline.
- [Check loop pressure](/quests/geothermal/check-loop-pressure) verifies system health.
- [Log ground temperature](/quests/geothermal/log-ground-temperature) builds data history.
- [Monitor heat pump energy](/quests/geothermal/monitor-heat-pump-energy) ties usage to output.

## Best practices

1. Calibrate sensors before trusting long-term data.
2. Keep a maintenance log for every purge, backflush, or component swap.
3. Compare seasonal readings to spot insulation or loop issues early.
4. Record energy usage alongside temperature changes for full context.
