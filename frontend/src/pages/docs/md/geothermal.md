Geothermal in DSPACE focuses on monitoring closed-loop systems: temperatures, pressures,
and flow. The quests mirror field maintenance and sensor calibration.

## What you will practice

- **Sensor checks**: calibrating ground sensors and logging readings.
- **Flow management**: inspecting loop pressure and inlet/outlet temperatures.
- **Maintenance**: backflushing filters and tracking system health.

## Quest flow highlights

Geothermal quests walk through loop pressure checks, inlet/outlet temperature verification,
sensor calibration, and backflushing the loop filter.

## Inventory highlights

- Temperature and pressure logs for geothermal loops.
- Calibration checklists and maintenance records.

## Why it matters

Geothermal is a long-term, stable energy source. Monitoring discipline here transfers to
other infrastructure systems and environmental control.
