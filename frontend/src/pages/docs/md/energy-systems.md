Energy systems quests expand beyond individual solar panels to cover storage, conversion, and
off-grid reliability.

## What you learn

- How to size batteries and inverters for real loads
- How to wire charge controllers and protect DC runs
- How to blend solar, wind, and manual generation for redundancy

## Quest trailheads

- [Configure a Solar Charge Controller](/quests/energy/charge-controller-setup)
- [Install a Power Inverter](/quests/energy/power-inverter)
- [Maintain Your Battery Pack](/quests/energy/battery-maintenance)
- [Install a Wind Turbine](/quests/energy/wind-turbine)
- [Charge a Device Off-Grid](/quests/energy/offgrid-charger)
- [Build a Hand Crank Generator](/quests/energy/hand-crank-generator)

## Key gear

- 200 Wh battery pack or 1 kWh battery pack for storage capacity goals
- 300 W pure sine inverter and Solar charge controller for conversion and charging
- Portable solar panel, 500 W wind turbine, and hand crank generator assembly for sources

## Common pitfalls

- Mixing battery chemistries or voltages without matching charge profiles
- Skipping fuses or undervaluing cable size on DC runs
- Tracking generation but not logging load usage over time
