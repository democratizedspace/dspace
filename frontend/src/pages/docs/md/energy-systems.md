Energy systems cover storage, conversion, and resilience across power sources. DSPACE quests mix
solar, wind, hand-crank, battery upkeep, and off-grid charging so you can build a dependable
energy stack.

## What this skill covers

- Battery maintenance, upgrades, and charge controller setups.
- Off-grid charging and inverter usage for real devices.
- Wind and manual generation for backup scenarios.
- Logging output for dSolar and dWatt milestones.

## Recommended inventory

- Battery packs, fused cabling, and a charge controller.
- Inverters and load testing tools for AC devices.
- Wind turbine kits or hand-crank generators for diversity.

## Quest highlights

- [Battery maintenance](/quests/energy/battery-maintenance) keeps storage healthy.
- [Power inverter](/quests/energy/power-inverter) connects DC to AC loads.
- [Wind turbine](/quests/energy/wind-turbine) adds a second generation source.
- [Hand crank generator](/quests/energy/hand-crank-generator) covers emergency power.

## Best practices

1. Track state-of-charge and cycle counts for every battery.
2. Keep spare fuses and monitor cable temperatures under load.
3. Diversify generation so single points of failure do not stop progress.
4. Log output in consistent units to compare sources over time.

## Where to go next

Energy systems complement solar power and electricity skills for full-stack power mastery.
